How frequently Affordable Mobiles flagged their reviews in the past 12 months

What's a flagged review?

  • Affordable Mobiles may flag a review if they think it's in breach of Trustpilot's guidelines.

  • Affordable Mobiles selects the reason(s) they're flagging the review, so we know what to investigate.

  • We don’t hide flagged reviews that are under investigation, unless they are flagged as having harmful or illegal content (then we blur them out).

  • Our Content Integrity Team assesses whether the flagged review is in fact in breach of our guidelines. If it is, we contact the reviewer.

  • If the reviewer is unable to resolve the issue, we remove the review from trustpilot.com.

  • Affordable Mobiles flagged 14 of their reviews a total of 14 times.
  • 42% of those cases turned out to be invalid.
